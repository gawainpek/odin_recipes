Fried Rice

Description

Classic fried rice that is sure to bring comfort to anyone who grew up in an Asian household.

Simple to make with leftover ingredients from the day before.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Eggs
  • Shrimp
  • Cooking oil
  • White pepper
  • Soy suace

Steps

  1. Heat oil in frying pan
  2. Add garlic, fry until golden brown
  3. Add shallots, fry until golden brown
  4. Add rice, mix with garlic and shallots
  5. Add eggs and stir fry
  6. Add white pepper and soy sauce, season to taste
