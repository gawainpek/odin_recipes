Iced Milo

Description

A comforting malt chocolate drink to go with your meal or as dessert.

Ingrdeients

  • Milo powder
  • Hot water
  • Milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Add 2-3 teaspoons of Milo powder into cup
  2. Add hot water until half full
  3. Stir evenly
  4. Top cup off with milk
  5. Stir evenly
